My Hero Academia (僕のヒーローアカデミア, My Hero Academia) is a manga series written and illustrated by Kōhei Horikoshi. The plot follows Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without superpowers (called quirks) in a world where they have become commonplace, but still dreams of becoming a hero. He meets All Might, Japan's greatest hero and symbol of peace, who passes on his Quirk to Izuku after acknowledging his potential
